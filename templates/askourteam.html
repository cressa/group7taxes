William E. Thomas, CPA - Partner

Our managing Partner, William has been with the firm since 1978 and became partner in 1983. William his wife and two daughters reside on the west side of Cleveland, Ohio.
William is a 1973 graduate of Westlake High School and a 1977 graduate of Wittenberg University. He is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants as well as the Ohio Society of Certified Public Accountants. William has served on various state committies for the Ohio Society of Certified Public Accountants as well as Local Cleveland committees. He also serves as an advisor to various boards of directors of local Cleveland companies and non profit organizations. Recently William was the President of the Lakewood/Rocky River Rotary club and is currently treasurer of North Coast Heath Ministry; Dover UCC Church in Westlake, Ohio, and Past President for the Lakewood/Rocky River Rotary Foundation. Mr. Thomas is also a trustee of Deaconess Community Foundation and the Pension Boards of The United church of Christ where he serves on the budget, healthcare and audit committees.
